			GALAGA User Manual



	Please use the following keys to operate this game:

'a' - moves the player left.
'd' - moves the player right.
's' - fires a projectile up.
'r' - initiates the enemies.
mouse click - pauses and resumes the game.

	Please note that in this game you cannot move the player up or down.

	The object of this game is to destroy all of the enemies before they
destroy you. Once you initiate the enemeis they will spawn on the screen, 
fire projectiles down at you and will go into an attack sequence while 
continuing to fire projectiles at you.
